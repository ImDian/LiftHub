How It Works

Our platform helps you calculate your daily calorie intake and deducts it by the calories you burn during workouts. The result? A clear view of your daily progress: deficit, surplus, or maintenance.

With meals and workouts already implemented, it’s easy to get started. Simply select your options and see your results in no time.
